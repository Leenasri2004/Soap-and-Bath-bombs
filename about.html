About Our Studio

Crafting Natural Beauty with Care

Where passion meets purity in every handmade soap and bath bomb, creating gentle skincare that loves your skin back.

Our Story

What started as a simple desire for chemical-free skincare has blossomed into Chennai's most beloved soap and bath bomb workshop studio. Founded in 2020, we began our journey in a small kitchen, experimenting with natural ingredients and traditional cold-process methods.

Our passion for handmade skincare grew from personal experience with sensitive skin and the realization that nature provides everything we need for healthy, glowing skin. Every bar we craft tells a story of careful ingredient selection, patient curing, and genuine love for the art of soap making.

Today, we're proud to share our knowledge through hands-on workshops, helping hundreds of people discover the joy of creating their own natural skincare products while building a community of conscious beauty enthusiasts.

Discover More

Our Mission

To make natural, handmade skincare accessible to everyone through education, workshops, and premium products. We believe everyone deserves to know what goes on their skin and have the power to create their own gentle, effective skincare solutions.

Our Vision

To become India's leading natural skincare education hub, inspiring a generation to choose conscious beauty. We envision a world where handmade, chemical-free skincare is the norm, not the exception, creating healthier communities and a cleaner planet.
