Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our workshops and services

Do I need any prior experience for workshops?

No experience needed at all! We start from basics and guide you step by step. Our workshops are designed for beginners and we provide all materials and instructions.

Can I place custom soap orders for events?

Absolutely! We create custom soaps for weddings, corporate gifts, and special events. Contact us with your requirements and we'll provide a quote. Minimum order quantities apply.

Do you deliver across Chennai?

Yes, we deliver our handcrafted soaps across Chennai. Free delivery on orders over ₹500. For workshop bookings, our studio is located in Thiruvanmiyur.

Are your soaps suitable for sensitive skin?

Many of our soaps are formulated for sensitive skin using natural, gentle ingredients. We avoid harsh chemicals and use cold-pressed oils. Contact us for specific recommendations.

What's the minimum age for workshop participation?

Kids above 7 years can join our workshops. For younger children, we suggest a parent or guardian to accompany them. Our bath bomb workshops are especially popular with families!

How do I confirm my workshop slot?

Call us at +91 8825826702 or WhatsApp us. We'll check availability and send you payment details to confirm your booking. We usually respond within a few hours.
